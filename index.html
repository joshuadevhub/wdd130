Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa and it's also known as the "Giant of Africa". Nigeria is a big and lively country in West Africa. It has the largest number of people in Africa, with many different cultures, languages, and traditions. Nigeria has a lot of natural resources like oil, and many people work in farming and business.
